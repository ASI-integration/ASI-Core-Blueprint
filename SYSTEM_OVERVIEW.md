# ASI System Overview

ASI (Autonomous Systems Integration) is a modular automation platform designed to manage and optimize operational workflows for distributed assets such as short-term rental properties. 
System structure map:

→ ASI_SYSTEM_MAP.md

The system architecture is organized around several operational contours:

## Core Operational Contours

1. Booking Intake  
Handles reservation ingestion from OTA channels and direct sources.

2. Occupancy Risk Evaluation  
Determines the operational risk level of incoming bookings.

3. Arrival Outcome Processing  
Decides operational actions based on booking and guest signals.

4. Finance Contour  
Handles revenue, ledger events, and operational financial state.

5. Communications Contour  
Manages guest communication workflows and messaging triggers.

6. Audit Memory & Accountability  
Maintains traceable operational history and decision logs.

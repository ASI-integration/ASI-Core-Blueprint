# WORKING RULES

## Communication Discipline
- Move in small steps.
- No multiple alternative strategies unless requested.
- No speculative expansions.
- Provide one concrete action at a time.
- Wait for confirmation before proceeding.

## Engineering Discipline
- Follow defined architecture.
- Do not invent new patterns without explicit request.
- Preserve system consistency.

## Context Discipline
- If context is unclear, ask.
- Do not assume hidden requirements.
